How It Works

Three easy steps — you focus on the move, we handle the rest.

1

We Deliver

Choose your package and move date. We'll drop off a stack of clean, sturdy totes and a dolly right at your door — the day before your move.

→
2

You Move

Pack at your own pace. Roll fully-loaded totes with your dolly — no heavy lifting needed. Take up to 3 days to complete your move.

→
3

We Pick Up

Leave the empty totes at your new place. We'll swing by within the agreed window and collect everything — so you can focus on settling in.

Why ToteMove?

Dolly Always Included

Every rental comes with a heavy-duty dolly — no renting it separately, no back strain.

Flat-Rate Pricing

Pay one price — delivery, pickup, and the dolly are all included. No per-day charges hiding in the fine print.

Proudly Local

Based in North Brampton. When you book with us, you're supporting a local small business — not a national chain.

Eco-Friendly

Reusable plastic totes mean zero cardboard waste — better for the environment, and sturdier for your stuff.

Uniform Stackable Totes

Flexible Scheduling
